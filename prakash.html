Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Prakash Easwaran is a semiconductor veteran with more than 28 years of experience in various semiconductor and systems companies like Texas Instruments, Cosmic Circuits, and InnoRel Systems. His expertise lies in building high-performance teams executing analog mixed projects in automotive, consumer, and networking domains. Prakash was the CTO of Cosmic Circuits before its successful acquisition by Cadence. Prakash has 5 papers and 29 patents attributed to his name.
