Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Prof Ashudeb Dutta (Member, IEEE) received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering, IIT Kharagpur, Kharagpur, India. He is currently an Associate Professor with IIT Hyderabad, Hyderabad, India. He has more than 19 years of research and development experience in the broad area of analog circuit, RFIC, semiconductor devices, energy harvesting, biomedical and healthcare, and smart security. He has had over 60 referred international conferences and journal publications.
